Scenario 1

You've just come home from the grocery store with a bucket of milk in one hand and your keys in the other. After changing your shoes you put the milk in the refrigerator, put the keys on the table, and go back to your bedroom. Ten minutes later, you return to the kitchen and find the milk on the table and the keys in the fridge - you've got them backwards!
